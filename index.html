How to Play


  1. 1) You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

  2. 2) There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total collected. Every time the game starts over the amount of points a crystal gives you will change

  3. 3) You win the game by matching your total score to random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

  4. 4) The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

  5. 5) Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
